Game Rules

  • No check. Simply capture the enemy king to win. The game continues as long as a player has at least 1 king and can make a move.
  • Different gamemodes (particularly 1D chess) may have different rules for piece movement. Clicking on a piece will highlight its legal moves.

Draft Rules

  • Players take turns drafting pieces that will be used in the game.
  • Each piece costs a certain number of points. Drafting a piece spends that many points.
